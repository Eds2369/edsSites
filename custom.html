Our Custom Web Design Process!

  1. Client contacts us for custom design
  2. We follow back with outline of whats needed. Client revises the outline
  3. We present the cost estimate
  4. Contract signature is required and we send an invoice of 50% down payment
  5. We present a preview of design
  6. Client gives any feedback and suggestions
  7. We revise client feedback and make changes to the design
  8. Client reviews changes and suggestions
  9. If client is happy with the design we will send over the files for website
  10. We will then guide on how to host your website online
